Call for Sponsorship

Organized biennially, the series of EUROLAN international summer schools is renown for bringing forward in each edition groundbreaking topics in the domain of Human Language Technologies. The EUROLAN-2017 Summer School will offer one week of intensive study in the emerging area of Biomedical Text Processing (Bio-NLP).

We are pleased to announce that the 13th edition of EUROLAN will be held in Constanța, Romania, jointly organized by Romanian Academy, Faculty of Computer Science (UAIC-FII) of "Alexandru Ioan Cuza" Universty of Iasi, Vassar College (USA) and Faculties of Medicine and of Mathematics and Computer Science of “Ovidius” University in Constanța – the host of this event.

As organizers of EUROLAN-2017, we are pleased to invite medicine and pharmacy companies, IT companies, local authorities, scientific research organizations, small enterprises and international firms to sponsor this major event and benefit from its international visibility, media coverage and economic repercussions.
